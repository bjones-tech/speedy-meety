About Speedy Meety

Do you find yourself in meetings that are scheduled longer than necessary? Most colleagues will exchange more information with one another in a quick hallway conversation than in a scheduled meeting. Speedy Meety is the way to have a quick and purposeful "hallway conversation" with people around the globe.

Speedy Meety's Features

  • Easily set the length of a meeting based on an efficient length of time, rather than a standard 30 or 60 minute time slot.
  • Provide multiple topics and Speedy Meety will evenly divide the time among them to form an agenda.
  • Receive a text transcription of the meeting's voice conversation to review any information that was shared.

Reference

Command Description
/meet5 Cycling, Running, ... Topics: Provide a list of topics separated by commas
/meet8 Baseball, Football Meeting Length: Set the meeting length (Range 1 - 30)
/meet! Movies, TV Shows, Music Automatic Meeting Length: Automatically sets the meeting length within the current 30 minute time slot
/meet? Greeting/Help: Display Speedy Meety's greeting message which includes the link to this web page
/next Skip to the next topic. Only available while a meeting is in progress
/status Display the status of the current topic. Only available while a meeting is in progress

Meeting Tips

  • List important topics closer to the beginning
  • Allow one person to speak at a time for a more efficient discussion and higher accuracy of transcription
  • Have a fun "rapid-fire" style discussion by setting up a shorter meeting with multiple topics
